About Us

Damru is not just the annual cultural fest of Rishihood University but a celebration of creativity, connection, and inspiration. This "Ten Years of Magic" has grown into a hub for transformative experiences, blending thrilling competitions, engaging activities, and performances by renowned artists. Over the years, Rishihood University has played host to a stellar lineup of influencers, parliamentarians, philanthropists, Padma Shri awardees, celebrities, senior academicians, and C Series guests. From electrifying concerts to thought-provoking discussions, Damru embodies the spirit of innovation, making lasting memories and fueling the dreams of all who join its vibrant journey.
