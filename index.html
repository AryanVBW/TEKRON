ABOUT US

Welcome to TEKRON, an electrifying celebration of technology, innovation, and creativity. Our fest brings together tech enthusiasts, problem solvers, and curious minds from all around to engage in a series of thrilling challenges. With events designed to push boundaries and spark collaboration, TEKRON promises an unforgettable experience for everyone involved. From the adrenaline-pumping Robo Soccer, where teams of robots face off in a high-stakes match, to the intense, non-stop action of the 24-Hour Hackathon, TEKRON offers something for every tech enthusiast. Test your skills in the globally recognized ICPC, or embark on a treasure hunt through our innovative Scavenger Hunt, filled with puzzles and riddles that will keep you on your toes. Whether you're coding, strategizing, or just having fun, TEKRON is the place where ideas come to life. Get ready for a journey full of excitement, challenges, and the chance to connect with like-minded individuals. Let the games begin!
