Event Structure

Participants

  • 10-15 players per game.
  • Roles:
    • 1 Imposter
    • 1 Jester
    • The rest are Crewmates

Game Setup

  • Each player will be given a role card (Imposter, Jester, or Crewmate) at the start of the game.
  • Walkie-talkies will be distributed to all players for communication.
  • Missions will be assigned to Crewmates (e.g., solving puzzles, finding hidden objects, etc.).
  • The Imposter will be given a list of fake missions to blend in.

Game Rules

  • Imposter: Can "kill" Crewmates by lightly tapping their back.
  • Jester: Must get themselves voted out during meetings to win.
  • Crewmates: Complete missions and identify the Imposter.
  • Walkie-Talkies: Can only be used to report dead bodies or call for emergency meetings.

Winning Conditions

  • Imposter: Wins if all Crewmates are eliminated or if they survive for 30 minutes.
  • Crewmates: Win if they vote out the Imposter.
  • Jester: Wins if they are voted out during a meeting.
